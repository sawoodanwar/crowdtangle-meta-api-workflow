# Data Ethics & Platform Terms of Service

## Key Principles

1. **Only collect public content** — do not attempt to access private profiles or groups
2. **Do not redistribute raw data** — share only aggregated or anonymized outputs
3. **Comply with Meta's ToS** — data may only be used for the approved research purpose
4. **Anonymize where needed** — remove or hash author identifiers before analysis
5. **Secure storage** — store raw data locally, never commit to public repositories

## IRB / Ethics Approval

Depending on your institution and country, social media data collection may require ethics board approval. Consult your institution's IRB or ethics committee before beginning data collection.

## Recommended Citation Practice

When publishing research using CrowdTangle or Meta Content Library data, acknowledge the data source clearly in your methods section.
